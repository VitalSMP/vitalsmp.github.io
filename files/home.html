Server Rules

Our rules keep VitalSMP welcoming, competitive, and fun for every player in the community.

1
Respect All Players

Treat everyone with respect in chat and gameplay to keep the community positive and enjoyable.

2
No Cheating or Hacking

Using hacks, x-ray, or unfair mods is strictly prohibited and results in immediate staff action.

3
No Griefing Spawn Areas

Protect shared spaces by leaving spawn and public builds safe for all players.

4
Follow Staff Instructions

Staff decisions are made to keep the server fair and stable, please cooperate when contacted.

5
No Offensive Builds or Names

Keep usernames, skins, and builds appropriate so everyone can enjoy a friendly environment.

6
Keep Chat Clean

No spam, harassment, or toxic behavior — keep conversations respectful and welcoming.

7
No Advertising Other Servers

Help grow VitalSMP by keeping promotions for external servers out of chat and player messages.

8
Have Fun!

Build, explore, and make friends — VitalSMP is all about creating unforgettable Minecraft moments.
